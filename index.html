What We Do

Our Services

01
Core Drilling

Precise, clean holes through concrete, brick, and masonry for electrical conduits, plumbing, and structural penetrations. Any diameter, any depth.

02
Demolition

Controlled structural and partial demolition for renovations and site preparation. Safe, efficient breakdown of walls, floors, and concrete structures.

03
Concrete Cutting

Straight, accurate cuts through reinforced and unreinforced concrete slabs, walls, and roads. Diamond-blade equipment for minimal vibration and dust.

04
Plumbing

Professional installation and repair of pipes, fixtures, and drainage systems. From new builds to plumbing repairs — done right the first time.
